## Overview

This directory contains research notes, literature review summaries, conceptual memos, and exploratory analyses produced during the research process. These documents are not peer-reviewed and should not be cited as authoritative sources. They represent work-in-progress thinking and serve as intellectual scaffolding for formal publications.

---

## Organisation

Notes are organised by research pillar:

```
research_notes/
├── pillar_I_data_governance/     # Notes on judicial data governance
├── pillar_II_digital_justice/    # Notes on digital justice and legal tech
├── pillar_III_ai_governance/     # Notes on AI governance and regulation
└── cross_cutting/                # Interdisciplinary and methodological notes
```

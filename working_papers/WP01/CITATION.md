## Preferred citation

Once the Zenodo DOI is issued, cite by DOI. Until then, cite by
version + repository URL.

## Version-of-record citation formats

### BibTeX

```bibtex
@techreport{djoro2026wp01,
  author      = {DJORO Yoboukoua, Lionel Arnaud},
  title       = {Data Governance as Foundational Infrastructure for
                 Intelligent Justice Systems in Africa: Toward
                 Human-Centered, Accountable and AI-Ready Judicial
                 Institutions},
  institution = {Data Governance for Intelligent Justice Systems
                 Research Programme},
  type        = {Working Paper},
  number      = {WP01},
  year        = {2026},
  month       = {jul},
  url         = {https://github.com/lioneldyla/judicial-data-ai-governance-portfolio/tree/main/working_papers/WP01},
  note        = {ORCID: 0009-0007-3762-9792. DOI pending Zenodo release.},
  version     = {1.0}
```

## Once Zenodo DOI is issued

Replace the `url` (BibTeX) or the trailing URL (APA/Chicago/MLA/Vancouver)
with `https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.XXXXXXX` and add `doi = {10.5281/zenodo.XXXXXXX}` to the
BibTeX entry.

## Once SSRN paper number is issued

Add the SSRN identifier as a `note` field in BibTeX and as a supplementary
identifier in the human-readable citations.

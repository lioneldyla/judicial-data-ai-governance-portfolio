## Overview

This document presents the structured research agenda for the DGIJS programme. It organises open research questions, prioritises investigation tracks, and maps questions to planned research outputs. The agenda is a living document, updated as knowledge accumulates and new questions emerge.

---

## Research Track 1 — Judicial Data Governance

### Central Question

What governance frameworks are necessary and sufficient to ensure that judicial data systems in African countries are reliable, secure, rights-respecting, and institutionally accountable?

### Sub-Questions

| ID | Question | Status | Planned Output |
|---|---|---|---|
| RQ1.1 | What are the defining characteristics of judicial data as a distinct category of public data? | In progress | WP01 |
| RQ1.2 | What governance gaps exist in current judicial data management practices in Côte d'Ivoire? | In progress | WP01, Case Study |
| RQ1.3 | How do FAIR principles apply to judicial data systems? | Planned | WP01 |
| RQ1.4 | What institutional arrangements are most effective for judicial data governance? | Planned | JA01 |
| RQ1.5 | How can a maturity model for judicial data governance be designed and validated? | Planned | JDGF v2.0 |

## Research Track 2 — Artificial Intelligence in Justice Systems

### Central Question

How should justice systems in Africa govern the adoption and use of artificial intelligence to ensure accountability, fairness, and rights-compatibility?

### Sub-Questions

| ID | Question | Status | Planned Output |
|---|---|---|---|
| RQ2.1 | What AI applications are currently used or planned in African justice systems? | Planned | WP02 |
| RQ2.2 | How does the EU AI Act apply to judicial AI systems, and what lessons can Africa draw? | Planned | WP02, JA02 |
| RQ2.3 | What regulatory frameworks are needed for judicial AI in OHADA member states? | Planned | Policy Brief |
| RQ2.4 | How can algorithmic accountability be institutionalised in judicial settings? | Planned | Doctoral Research |
| RQ2.5 | What are the human rights implications of predictive tools in criminal justice? | Planned | JA03 |

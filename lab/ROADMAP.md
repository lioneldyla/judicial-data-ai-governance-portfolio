## Review policy

- **Reviewed:** at every doctoral milestone (annual until doctoral registration, per-phase thereafter).
- **Bumped:** in `CHANGELOG.md` when a milestone moves by more than one quarter.
- **Never:** back-dated to hide a slipped deadline. A slipped milestone is documented, explained, and re-projected.

*This roadmap is intentionally ambitious. Any milestone whose feasibility
becomes unclear is flagged in the next annual review rather than
silently dropped.*

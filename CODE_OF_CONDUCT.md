# Code of Conduct

**Judicial Data & AI Governance Research Portfolio**

> Version: 1.0 | Author: DJORO Yoboukoua Lionel Arnaud | Last updated: 2026-07-06

---

## Our Commitment

The Judicial Data & AI Governance Research Portfolio is committed to fostering an open, respectful, inclusive, and collaborative research environment. We welcome contributions, discussions, and collaborations from researchers, practitioners, public institutions, students, and members of the broader scientific community — regardless of nationality, gender, ethnicity, religion, disability, age, or professional background.

Our objective is to promote **scientific integrity**, **responsible innovation**, and **constructive dialogue** in the fields of Judicial Data Governance, Digital Justice, Artificial Intelligence Governance, and Digital Public Transformation.

---

## Expected Behaviour

All participants are expected to:

- Treat others with respect, professionalism, and courtesy
- Engage in constructive and evidence-based discussions
- Support diversity of perspectives and interdisciplinary collaboration
- Cite sources accurately and acknowledge intellectual contributions
- Respect confidentiality where applicable
- Encourage responsible and ethical use of data and artificial intelligence
- Follow applicable laws, institutional policies, and academic integrity standards

---

## Unacceptable Behaviour

The following behaviours are not acceptable:

- Harassment or discrimination of any kind
- Personal attacks, insults, or intimidation
- Plagiarism or misrepresentation of research
- Deliberate dissemination of false or misleading information
- Sharing confidential or restricted information without authorization
- Violating intellectual property or licensing terms
- Any activity that undermines scientific integrity or respectful collaboration

---

## Scientific Integrity

This repository promotes:

- Research transparency
- Reproducibility whenever possible
- Responsible citation practices
- Open Science principles
- FAIR data principles where applicable
- Responsible Artificial Intelligence

Contributors are encouraged to clearly distinguish between established evidence, interpretation, and opinion.

---

## Collaboration Principles

Contributions should:

- Improve scientific quality
- Enhance clarity and reproducibility
- Respect existing repository organization
- Be documented appropriately
- Follow the repository contribution guidelines

Major research changes should be discussed before implementation.

---

## Responsible AI

Research involving Artificial Intelligence should:

- Respect human rights
- Promote accountability
- Reduce bias where possible
- Protect privacy and sensitive information
- Support human-centered decision-making
- Comply with applicable ethical and legal standards

---

## Reporting Concerns

If you observe behaviour inconsistent with this Code of Conduct, please contact the repository maintainer through the contact information provided in the repository profile or [ORCID record](https://orcid.org/0009-0007-3762-9792).

Reports will be handled respectfully, confidentially, and in good faith.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- GitHub Issues
- Pull Requests
- Discussions
- Repository documentation
- Research collaborations directly associated with this repository

## Acknowledgement

This Code of Conduct is inspired by internationally recognized open-source and research community practices and has been adapted to reflect the values of academic integrity, responsible innovation, and open scientific collaboration.

## The publication logic

Publications flow through a defined pipeline:

```
Working Paper ─► Zenodo DOI ─► SSRN preprint ─► Peer review ─► Journal article ─► Book chapter ─► Book
                                                                                                    │
                                                                                                    ▼
                                                                             Policy briefs · Technical notes · Presentations
```

At each stage the artefact is versioned, licensed under CC BY 4.0, and
cross-referenced from the Portfolio's citation register
([`../CITATION.cff`](../CITATION.cff)).

*Source: [`../PUBLICATION_POLICY.md`](../PUBLICATION_POLICY.md).*

## Reproducibility and open science

All Working Papers and Journal Articles are:

- deposited on Zenodo with a version DOI (once the manuscript is complete
  — see [`../working_papers/WP01/RELEASE_CHECKLIST.md`](../working_papers/WP01/RELEASE_CHECKLIST.md));
- registered against the researcher's ORCID
  ([0009-0007-3762-9792](https://orcid.org/0009-0007-3762-9792));
- indexed on SSRN as preprints where policy allows;
- licensed under CC BY 4.0;
- accompanied by a Data Availability Statement pointing at any
  associated dataset in [`../datasets/`](../datasets/).

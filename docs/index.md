## Research Programme

**Data Governance for Intelligent Justice Systems**

This programme explores how governance, data, and artificial intelligence can modernize judicial institutions while preserving transparency, accountability, ethics, and the rule of law.

The programme articulates around three interdependent pillars:

| Pillar | Theme | Horizon |
|--------|-------|---------|
| **I — Data Governance** | Judicial data governance frameworks, data quality, judicial statistics | 2026–2028 |
| **II — Digital Justice** | Digital transformation of court administration, e-justice, registry modernisation | 2026–2029 |
| **III — AI Governance** | Responsible AI in judicial decision-making, algorithmic accountability | 2026–2035 |

## Once references are added

Each entry follows APA 7th-edition format. Example (with placeholder
fields; real entries will replace this):

```
Family, A. B. (YYYY). Title of the work. Publisher.
    https://doi.org/10.xxxx/yyyy
```

Alphabetical order by first-author family name, with each section
independently ordered.

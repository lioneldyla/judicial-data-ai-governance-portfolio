## Overview

This directory contains research datasets associated with the **Data Governance for Intelligent Justice Systems** research programme.

Datasets will be published whenever legally, ethically, and institutionally appropriate. All datasets follow **FAIR principles** (Findable, Accessible, Interoperable, Reusable) and apply responsible data governance standards.

## Data Governance Principles

All datasets in this repository:

- Comply with applicable legal and ethical requirements
- Are anonymized where personal or sensitive information is involved
- Include complete metadata and documentation
- Specify reuse conditions and licensing
- Document provenance and methodology
- Sensitive judicial data is **never** released without proper authorization

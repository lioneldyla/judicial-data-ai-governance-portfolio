## Overview

This directory contains all peer-reviewed journal articles produced within the **Data Governance for Intelligent Justice Systems** research programme.

Journal articles represent the highest-impact scientific outputs of the programme. They are derived from working papers, research frameworks, and empirical studies, and are submitted to internationally recognized academic journals.

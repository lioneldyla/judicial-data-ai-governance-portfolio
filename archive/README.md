# Archive

## Judicial Data & AI Governance Research Portfolio

> Layer 13 — Long-Term Preservation | Research Digital Infrastructure (RDI)

---

## Overview

This directory contains archived versions of research outputs, superseded documents, legacy materials, and older versions preserved for reproducibility and historical traceability within the **Data Governance for Intelligent Justice Systems** research programme.

---

## Purpose

The archive ensures:
- **Reproducibility** — Previous versions of documents remain accessible
- **Traceability** — Evolution of research outputs is documented
- **Historical record** — Superseded materials are preserved, not deleted
- **Long-term accessibility** — Archived outputs remain citable

---

## Archiving Policy

- Documents are archived when superseded by a new version
- Original filenames are preserved with version suffixes
- Archived documents are never modified
- Each archived item includes its original metadata
- Zenodo DOIs ensure permanent external archiving of major releases

## Relationship with Zenodo

Major versions of all research outputs are also archived on **Zenodo** for:

- FAIR-compliant external archiving

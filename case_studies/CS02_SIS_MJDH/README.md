## 1. Context

SIS-MJDH is the statistical information system of the Ministry of
Justice and Human Rights (Ministère de la Justice et des Droits de
l'Homme) of Côte d'Ivoire. It is the primary infrastructure through
which judicial statistics are produced, consolidated, and disseminated
at the national level.

The researcher was Head of the Statistics Unit at MJDH (2018–2021) and
observed the SIS-MJDH's design, operation, and institutional constraints
at close range. This positionality is a scientific asset for the case
study and is disclosed here as required by
[`../../RESEARCH_ETHICS.md`](../../RESEARCH_ETHICS.md).

## 2. Problem

- Judicial statistics production in Côte d'Ivoire faces recurrent
  challenges: fragmented data collection at court level, uneven data
  quality, delayed consolidation, limited feedback to producing courts.
- The governance framework for SIS-MJDH — roles, responsibilities,
  quality standards — is partially formalized.
- The system exemplifies the kind of institutional data ecosystem that
  the JDGF is designed to support.

## 3. Methodology

Deep single-case study, applied research design:

1. **Institutional analysis** — mapping of governance structures,
   information flows, quality processes.
2. **Documentary analysis** — official documents, methodological
   guides, yearbooks, internal reports (where accessible).
3. **Semi-structured interviews** — judicial administrators,
   statistical officers, court clerks, IT officers.
4. **Framework application** — apply JDGF v1.0 to SIS-MJDH; identify
   gaps; measure maturity across the 8 domains; refine the framework
   iteratively.
5. **Feedback loop** — findings shared with the MJDH; institutional
   response documented as part of the case.

Positionality management:

- The researcher's prior role provides access and contextual knowledge.
- To mitigate observer bias, findings are cross-checked against
  interview data from actors the researcher did not directly work with.
- Interpretations that could be shaped by the researcher's own prior
  choices are explicitly flagged.

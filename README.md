## 🌍 Research Vision

This repository constitutes the **official open research portfolio** of **DJORO Yoboukoua Lionel Arnaud**, researcher in Judicial Data Governance, Digital Justice, and Responsible AI for the African public sector.

The overarching research vision is to design, validate, and disseminate **evidence-based frameworks** enabling African judiciaries to govern data responsibly, integrate AI ethically, and achieve measurable improvements in access to justice, transparency, and institutional integrity — consistent with the rule of law and African continental integration objectives (AU Agenda 2063, ECOWAS Digital Agenda).

This portfolio adheres to the **FAIR principles** (Findable, Accessible, Interoperable, Reusable) and is designed for integration with ORCID, Zenodo, SSRN, and GitHub Pages.

## 📐 Research Programme

The research programme articulates around three interdependent pillars:

| Pillar | Theme | Horizon |
|--------|-------|----------|
| **I — Data Governance** | Judicial data governance frameworks, data quality, judicial statistics, open data in justice | 2025–2027 |
| **II — Digital Justice** | Digital transformation of court administration, e-justice, registry modernisation, legal tech | 2025–2028 |
| **III — AI Governance** | Responsible AI in judicial decision-making, algorithmic accountability, AI regulation for African public sectors | 2026–2030 |

## 🔬 Research Areas

- **Judicial Data Governance** — data quality frameworks, judicial statistics systems, national judicial data infrastructure
- **Digital Justice & Legal Technology** — court digitisation, e-registry, case management systems, access to justice through technology
- **Responsible AI in Public Sector** — algorithmic accountability, AI ethics, AI regulatory frameworks for Africa
- **African Justice Systems Reform** — OHADA law, ECOWAS integration, institutional reform, capacity building
- **Open Science & Open Data** — FAIR data principles applied to legal and judicial datasets
- **Doctoral Research (Forthcoming)** — AI Governance & Data Governance (PhD in preparation)

## 🏗️ Research Frameworks

### JDGF — Judicial Data Governance Framework

> *Judicial Data Governance Framework for African Judiciaries*

The **JDGF** is an original multi-dimensional framework designed to enable African judiciaries to implement structured, responsible, and sustainable data governance. It integrates institutional, technical, legal, and ethical dimensions.

→ [Full framework documentation](frameworks/JDGF/)

### SPJEJ — Statistical Performance Index for Justice Efficiency in Justice

> *Statistical Performance Index for Justice Efficiency — Justice (SPJEJ)*

A composite index designed to measure judicial statistical performance across African court systems.

→ Planned — 2026 Q4

### CS GREFFE OS — Open Source Court Registry System

> *Système de Greffe Open Source*

An open-source digital registry platform for African courts, designed for low-resource deployment.

→ Planned — 2026 Q4

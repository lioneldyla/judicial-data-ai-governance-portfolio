## Thematic Clusters

### Cluster A — Judicial Data Governance
Judicial statistics, court administration reform, judicial information systems, data quality in justice.

### Cluster B — Digital Justice and Legal Technology
E-justice, court digitalisation, case management systems, access to justice through technology.

### Cluster C — AI Governance and Regulation
Algorithmic accountability, AI ethics, AI regulatory frameworks, responsible AI in public sector.

### Cluster D — African Context
African judiciaries, OHADA law, AU digital frameworks, ECOWAS digital agenda, justice reform in Africa.

### Cluster E — Methodology
Comparative law methodology, socio-legal research, institutional analysis, data science for law.

## Citation Standards

The programme uses **APA 7th Edition** as its primary citation style. Authors wishing to contribute references should follow:

- Full author names (not initials where available)
- DOI or stable URL for all digital sources
- Access dates for online documents without stable identifiers
- Language of publication noted for non-English sources

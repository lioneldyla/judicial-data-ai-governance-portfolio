## Research Programme

This portfolio documents the *Data Governance for Intelligent Justice Systems* research programme — an independent research initiative grounded in 15+ years of practical experience in judicial administration in Côte d'Ivoire and the broader West African judicial ecosystem.

The programme articulates around three interdependent research pillars:

| Pillar | Theme | Horizon |
|---|---|---|
| **I — Data Governance** | Judicial data governance frameworks, data quality, judicial statistics | 2025–2027 |
| **II — Digital Justice** | Court digitalisation, e-justice, registry modernisation, legal technology | 2025–2028 |
| **III — AI Governance** | Responsible AI in judicial decision-making, algorithmic accountability, AI regulation | 2026–2030 |

## Central question (doctoral)

**RQ0.** How can judicial institutions in Africa govern judicial data and
artificial intelligence systems in ways that strengthen rather than
undermine justice, accountability, and the rule of law?

## Review policy

The question register is reviewed annually and at every doctoral
milestone review. A question can be:

- **Added** — new open problem identified during research;
- **Reprioritised** — status changed (Planned → Active → Resolved);
- **Marked resolved** — sufficient evidence has been produced and
  peer-reviewed;
- **Retired** — determined to be off-programme or better handled elsewhere.

All changes are recorded in
[`../CHANGELOG.md`](../CHANGELOG.md) and, if substantive, in
[`../governance/implementations/`](../governance/implementations/).

## About

I am a researcher and justice sector professional working at the intersection of governance, digital transformation, and artificial intelligence. My work focuses on designing governance frameworks, digital public infrastructures, and evidence-based methodologies that strengthen judicial institutions and support the responsible adoption of AI in justice systems.

My long-term objective is to contribute to the development of **trusted, accountable, human-centered, and AI-ready justice systems**, particularly in Africa.

---

## Research Programme

### Data Governance for Intelligent Justice Systems

This programme explores how governance, data, and artificial intelligence can modernize judicial institutions while preserving transparency, accountability, ethics, and the rule of law. The programme combines:

- Academic research
- Policy analysis
- Open science
- Research software
- Institutional innovation
- Public sector digital transformation

- Evidence-Based Justice
- Responsible AI

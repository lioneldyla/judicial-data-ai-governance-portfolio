## Abstract

*To be written.*

This working paper will develop an AI Readiness Assessment Framework specifically designed for justice institutions in Africa. It will build upon WP01’s foundational infrastructure analysis to propose a structured methodology for evaluating institutional capacity, data governance maturity, and organizational readiness for responsible AI adoption.

## Research Questions

1. What dimensions define AI readiness in judicial institutions?
2. How can readiness be assessed across different institutional contexts in Africa?
3. What governance conditions are necessary for responsible AI adoption in justice systems?
4. How does data governance maturity relate to AI readiness?

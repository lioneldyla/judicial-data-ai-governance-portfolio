## Submission Standards

All articles submitted from this programme must:

1. Include a **Data Availability Statement** referencing the Zenodo repository
2. Cite the programme's CITATION.cff-compliant citation metadata
3. Adhere to the [Publication Policy](../../PUBLICATION_POLICY.md)
4. Follow [Research Ethics](../../RESEARCH_ETHICS.md) guidelines
5. Acknowledge the ORCID profile: [0009-0007-3691-9953](https://orcid.org/0009-0007-3691-9953)

---

## Open Access Policy

All articles are published open access wherever possible. When publisher agreements preclude gold open access, the accepted manuscript (post-peer review, pre-copyediting) will be deposited in Zenodo within 12 months of publication.

*See [Open Science Policy](../../OPEN_SCIENCE_POLICY.md) for full details.*
